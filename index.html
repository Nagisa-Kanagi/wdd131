West Jordan, Utah

West Jordan received its name from the Latter-Day Saint settlers who entered the Salt Lake Valley in 1847. West Jordan was founded around 1849 on the western banks of the Jordan River. West Jordan has a high religious population of 75.4%. The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints is the major faith, at 61% of the religious population.
